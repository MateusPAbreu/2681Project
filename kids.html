This creative writing class for 7th-8th grade girls and gender-expansive youth is focused on building self-awareness, Emotional and Social Intelligence (ESI), story intelligence, and intuitive self-confidence—while also having a lot of fun!

In this playful environment, students learn to find joy and self-mastery in the stories and worlds they create. It's also a safe space for students to address any personal challenges that make come up through the writing or group discussion. Each session involves an inspirational content, imaginative writing prompts, and time to write, share, and receive feedback.

The Intuitive Writing Project philosophy is rooted in the belief that everyone is a storyteller with their own unique voice. We support the development of one’s voice by focusing on the strengths of the writing. The more students write and share, the more they learn to believe in themselves, both as writers and as people.

Note: If your daughter already loves to write, we can support her emerging creative voice. If she doesn't (yet) enjoy writing, she will get to experience how fun, cathartic, and empowering it is.
