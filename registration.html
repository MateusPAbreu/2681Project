Registration

If you're new to Intuitive Writing and aren't sure what class to choose...

If you're new to The Intuitive Writing Project, I’d love to schedule a quick, 10-minute chat!

I am here to listen to and support to your daughter’s authentic self-expression, to celebrate her brilliance, and to hold space for her continuous self-discovery and growth.

This is the work I was born to do, what I call "my soul work," so connection is everything—and starts with YOU!

If you are new to The Intuitive Writing Project, I’d love to schedule a short, 10-minute conversation, just to connect in person. I want to give you the chance to meet me, to be able to ask questions, and to make sure our programs are a good fit for your daughter. I also want to learn from you about your daughter’s unique interests and needs, and what you'd like her to be focusing on right now.

Together, we can discuss which program might best support your daughter. And as you will hear me say a lot, your daughter is welcome to try a free intro class anytime. If she doesn't absolutely love her first class—I mean really, really love it—we want her to feel empowered to say no and focus her energy on something else.

Please throw out a few days and times that would be good for a quick call (maybe while you’re in the car or out for a walk so it’s easy) and we’ll get something on the calendar. And whenever you're ready to register, you can visit us here.

— Elizabeth
