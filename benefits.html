Benefits

A list for parents

  1. The Intuitive Writing Project offers an antidote to social media culture.
    As girls learn to own their story and discover their unique values, strengths, and passions, they develop a foundation of self-awareness and self-esteem that becomes protective armor against the adverse influences of social media. Our programs also provide the community and social connection that supports girls' overall happiness and well-being.
  2. We build critical thinking skills in an AI world.
    In learning to articulate and organize their thoughts, girls develop the critical thinking skills to solve complex problems and make smart choices. In this way, girls are empowered to find their answers through their own intuitive intelligence.
  3. We offer an empowering refuge from the pressure to be "perfect" so girls can grow more confident in their authenticity.
  4. We guide girls to discover and articulate their unique talents, strengths, passions, and values, while helping them connect-the-dots to specific college majors and careers.
  5. We help girls develop "story-intelligence," the ability to own their story and advocate for themselves in all aspects of life, including college, jobs, internship applications, and leadership positions in their community.
  6. Through writing, girls learn proven tools to manage stress and anxiety, become more focused and relaxed, and develop the resilience and optimism they need to take charge of their lives and their futures.
  7. Through class discussion, girls learn to be skilled and insightful conversationalists, as we explore the real-world, global events that impact their lives most, asking deep questions and encouraging open dialogue.
  8. From sharing ideas to offering peer feedback, girls learn to speak up with confidence, clarity, and respect in all aspects of their lives.
  9. Whether or not they started out liking writing, girls learn to love itâ€”and to become exceptionally talented writers, a skill which will set them apart everywhere from college apps to job interviews.
  10. In their teacher, girls have someone to talk to about things they can't discuss anywhere else, someone who will listen with empathy and guide them back to their own wisdom.
  11. Among their peer-group, girls experience the peace and power of being part of a supportive community, one that always has their back and believes in their voice.

A list for teens

  1. Even though girls describe our programs as "the opposite of school," they will help you feel more confident at school, speaking up in class, and everywhere you go, no matter who you're with.
  2. Discover your talents, strengths, values, and passions so you can make the best decisions and define success on your own terms.
  3. Connect to your intuitive superpowers through writing, a life-hack that will empower you to find your own answers and handle whatever the world throws at you, as the leader of your own life.
  4. Learn to communicate more effectively both at school and at home, to be able to say what you want and get what you need!
  5. Impress colleges and future employers because you know who you are and how to tell your story in a powerful way.
  6. Find the most compatible friends because you know what you value and how to advocate for yourself.
  7. Learn to resolve conflict and develop healthy boundaries in order to cultivate healthy relationships in all aspects of your life.
  8. Get clear about how you want to contribute to the world in alignment with your interests, strengths, and what brings you joy!
  9. Learn how to stand in your integrity and express yourself authentically and responsibly in digital spaces,immune from outside pressure.
  10. Have someone to talk to about things you can't discuss anywhere else, someone who will listen with empathy and guide you back to your own wisdom.
  11. Know the beauty, strength, and power that exists inside of you, a power that will only grow greater with time and can never be diminished by external events or changing circumstances.
  12. Know that who you are matters, that everything you think, feel, say, and do MATTERS, that you are unconditionally supported by a community of like-minded people who have your back and believe in your power.
  13. Know that the more you write, the more you will discover your unique beauty, magic, and powerâ€”and the more you will realize you are wise and amazing and can always trust yourself.
