10 Powerful Ways The Intuitive Writing Project Offers a Creative Antidote to the Mental Health Crisis Affecting Young Women

  1. In a world that mostly discounts or disrespects the insights of young people, The Intuitive Writing Project is here to listen to, honor, and respect their wisdom.
  2. In a social-media-and-AI-dominated world, we give girls the time and space they need to cultivate their own thoughts and connect to their own wisdom, strengthening their own intuitive intelligence.
  3. A surface-focused culture, The Intuitive Writing Project is here to offer young women the time and space they need to look within and have their stories heard, valued and validated.
  4. In a culture that prioritizes external achievement and "perfection," perpetuating a chronic sense of inadequacy, The Intuitive Writing Project prioritizes authentic self-expression and intuitive self-trust.
  5. Whereas girls are conditioned to focus on their external appearance, The Intuitive Writing Project focuses on inner strength and introspection.
  6. Whereas girls expect to be judged and ranked, The Intuitive Writing Project insists that everyone is equally respected, valued, and celebrated.
  7. Whereas girls are often expected to override or ignore their feelings, The Intuitive Writing Project offers a safe, structured space to process and grow from every experience, building emotional resilience and the knowledge that you will be able to handle whatever life throws at you.
  8. Whereas most young people struggle to know who they are, The Intuitive Writing Project supports each each girl in connecting to their unique talents, strengths, values and passions and having the confidence to pursue them.
  9. Whereas it is easy to feel despair about the state of the world, The Intuitive Writing Project fosters optimism and proactive engagement, ensuring that each young woman is empowered to advocate for the causes they care about most.
