Books I love & recommend

“Reading is breathing in; writing is breathing out.”
—Pam Allyn

Since The Intuitive Writing Project represents the synthesis of a lifetime of reading, study, and personal reclamation work, I’d like to share some of the books I’ve loved, quoted from in class, and highly recommend. It’s impossible to pick the wrong book. Just trust your intuition and let your curiosity lead you!
