College Essay Support

Tell your story. Find your voice.

Like all our writing programs, coaching for the college essay supports young people in finding their own authentic voice. We do this by creating a safe space for young people to share their experiences. Then we reflect back what we hear, affirming their individuality, their strengths, and their values as authors of their own life story. In this way, we are able to focus on the qualities colleges value most, including independent thought, self-awareness, resilience, emotional intelligence and leadership.

This kind of essay coaching is about helping your child think creatively and strategically about the most important experiences of their life—and then how to tell their story in a brave, authentic way. Ultimately, our focus is on supporting kids holistically, to help each young person speak their truth and feel confident about who they are and where they're headed.

We are proud to say that every student who’s written with us has been accepted into one of their first-choice schools. You can read about some of the great schools our graduates attend—and hear their thoughts on the benefits of writing to get them into college here.

Truly, a well-told story can change your life.​

To discuss your child's unique interests and goals, contact Elizabeth.

  • During the first session, we talk through, outline, and complete a first draft of the personal essay, which usually takes two hours.
  • Subsequent sessions (especially with supplemental essays) are usually completed in one hour.
  • Cost is $150 per hour.
  • Edits and revisions are offered via Google docs for FREE!
  • The pace of our meetings will be determined by your child.
