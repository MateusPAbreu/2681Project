Tuition

The Intuitive Writing Project is dedicated to serving every young person who loves to write and wants to write with us.

The transformative power of our programs comes from our low student-to-teacher ratio, with 4-6 writers max. This reflects the true cost of our programs. But because we want everyone who wants to write with us to be able to do so, we also offer scholarships. If tuition is a hardship for your family, please contact us to apply for a need-based scholarships.

Refund policy: If your child doesn't absolutely love their first class, just let us know before the second class and we will provide a full refund. We want your child to choose writing, purely for the joy of it!

Thank you for investing in our mission, empowering young writers to tell their story and find their voice.

Small Group Classes:
4-6 writers/class for individualized mentoring and support
  • Grade school programs: 60-75 minutes: $40/class
  • Middle school programs: 90 minutes: $70/class
  • High school programs: 2 hours: $90/class
1:1 Story Coaching:
Private writing support, Zoom only
  • Middle school age with Gretchen: $80/session
  • High school age with Elizabeth: $100/session
